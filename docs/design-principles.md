# Design Principles

## Visual Direction

Inspired by:

- McKinsey
- Bloomberg
- Gartner

## Core Principles

### Content First

Content should be the focus.

### Modular Design

Every section can be replaced independently.

### Dark and Light Themes

Support both themes.

### High-end Research Feel

Premium
Professional
Executive Audience

### Conversion-oriented

Every page should guide users to:

- Explore
- Register
- Subscribe
- Purchase
